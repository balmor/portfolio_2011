A little bit about me.

Personal Summary

Hello. Firstly I'd like to thank you for taking the time to visit the balmor website. My name is Damian Duda and I'm a 29 year old Graphic Designer and Web Designer based in Tychy, southern Poland. I have over five years experience in building and developing websites. I work freelance under the pseudonym "balmor".

I design with Photoshop, Dreamweaver and I code sites with HTML5 and CSS3, using a little of JavaScript, Actionscript, and PHP, and a lot of Usability, Typography, and Web Standards. All my work is made from scratch - I don't work with ready templates unless you want me to.

Outside Of The Office

In my free time, I enjoy reading good books, watching action movies and planning my global takeover. I often ride a bike and swim in the pool.
